Build Conversational Interfaces With DigiSensei

DigiSensei is a Zimbabwe-based conversation design studio. We pride ourselves on being able to integrate chatbots with all the tools that make you productive. We create chatbots for your staff, clients and stakeholders.

We build chatbots that serve a clearly defined purpose, make things easier and have a human touch. Our chatbots are easy to use and can learn, which motivates users to talk.

Key Features

DigiSensei Chatbots with plenty of awesome features to
wow the users.

Multi-Channel

We can connect your chatbot to a number of channels including your website, facebook page, telegram account, slack channel, microsoft teams, twillio and just about evry other api enabled interface.

Built-in NLU

With just a few question and answer pairs, we can train your chatbot to understand free speech and respond. You can also extract important information like dates times, products and more from what the user says with your chatbot

Metrics Tracking

We deliver intuitive dashboards that show usage statistics and custom deep dives to detect behaviours, trends and bottlenecks for continuous improvement of your chatbot.

Affordable Hosting

We provide affordable hosting as a service and ensure 95% up-time for your chatbot making us an ideal choice if you do not have a technical team which deals with server infrastructure.

Security & Confidentiality

Our chatbots are run on secure server infrastructure and we offer full non-disclosure of all information divulged to us by our customers.

e-Commerce Ready

Our chatbots can easily be integrated to any payment gateway of your choice and act as a shopping guide, following visitors around your website.
